Edisto Basin Planning

Activities and reports on water planning in the Edisto River basin.

Overview

The new South Carolina State Water Planning Framework is currently being implemented in the Edisto Basin. Two public meetings were held on November 18th and 21st, 2019 to introduce the new Planning Framework to stakeholders and to solicit River Basin Council Membership applications (presentations given at each meeting can be accessed on the meetings page). River Basin Council members were appointed by SCDNR in March 2020 (see the council page for a list of members) and CDM Smith, Inc. was awarded a contract to provide facilitation and authorship services for the Council. Formal Council meetings began in June, 2020, and meeting agendas, presentations, recordings, and meeting summaries can be accessed here. Please visit this website for periodic updates and new information regarding Edisto River basin planning activities.

Public Notice: Next Edisto River Basin Council Meeting Scheduled for October 20th, 2021 from 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM (Agenda)

The Council will meet in-person at the Clemson Edisto Research and Education Center in Blackville, SC. The public may attend in-person or virtually. Please contact Scott Harder (harders@dnr.sc.gov) for virtual meeting access information.

SCDNR is accepting Edisto River Basin Council membership applications for the Industry and Economic Development, and Water-based Recreational interest categories.

Interested stakeholders should complete an application form (submission instructions included in the application).
